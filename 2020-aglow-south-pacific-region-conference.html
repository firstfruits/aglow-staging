Aglow South Pacific Region Conference 2020

About the conference

22-24 July 2020 at the Sheraton Fiji Resort, Denarau Island, Nadi, Fiji.

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and all Judea and Samaria, and to THE ENDS OF THE EARTH" - Acts 1:8

Join us as the nations of the South Pacific including American Samoa, Australia, Fiji, New Zealand, Papua New Guinea, Western Samoa, Tonga and Vanuatu come together for the first Aglow South Pacific Region Conference.It is the right time for the nations of the South Pacific to gather together to propel the Kingdom of God forward, all the way to the ends of the earth.

Main Speakers

Plus other special guests
Joan bennett: director of the asia/pacific region - Aglow International
More to be announced

Nancy McDaniel

Nancy McDaniel serves as the Prayer Director of Aglow International. Nancy has been teaching on the power of Proclamation and Kingdom intercession. It is her desire to see the glory of God arising in the Nations and to partner with God to propel His Kingdom forward. Nancy says, “We have been appointed for a time such as this to bring a spiritual invasion of heaven’s rule over the governments of the earth”.

Pastor John Moala

John Moala is the Pastor of Walk by Faith Church International. It is one of the most influential churches in the Kingdom of Tonga. He is also a member of the advisory board of Aglow Tonga. John is a revivalist; he has spoken in many different arenas including hosting national revival crusades in Tonga. He and His wife Ana desire to see the power of the Holy Spirit transform every generation. Many people have been touched and equipped through their ministry and the Kingdom of God is being advanced.

Information

Location and Travel

Sheraton Fiji  Resort, Denarau Island, Fiji.25 minutes drive from Nadi airport.If flying into Nadi, we recommend you pre-book a shuttle to travel from Nadi airport to your chosen accommodation.

Cost

Registration cost is $250 Fijiian Dollars (FJD) Registration is available online: Register Now- please note online registration is in Australian Dollars (AUD).Paper registration is available in some countries. Please contact the Aglow office in your country for more information.

Accommodation

Book your own. Below is a list of nearby accommodation to assist in your research. The Sheraton has given Aglow a discounted rate which you can access using the link below. Be sure to look at hotel booking websites (eg booking.com, wotif etc) to find the best deals if you wish to stay at a different hotel.

Schedule

Conference officially opens Wednesday 22nd July in the evening, however there is a pre-conference session on Wednesday during the day. Details of this session are coming soon.Conference concludes on Friday 24th July in the evening.Further session details are below.

Meals

Light refreshments are provided following the pre-conference session on the morning of Wednesday 22nd JulyAfternoon tea is provided on Thursday 23th JulyLunch is provided on Friday 24th JulyFor all other meals, please arrange your own.

Planning your trip

If you are coming to the conference from outside of Fiji, we recommend you consult your doctor regarding your health and whether any vaccinations are recommended. You can also consult travel information websites (eg Smart Traveller)  for the latest news and information regarding overseas travel.
