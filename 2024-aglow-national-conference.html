Jeanette Ward

Jeanette Ward has been National President Aglow International New Zealand, Samoa and Tonga, since December 2010.  And 3 ½ years ago was appointed Chairperson for Aglow South Pacific.

Jeanette studied at Bible College NZ for 4 years and graduated with a Ministry Internship Diploma. For many years she was actively involved in leadership within her local church until God opened doors for her to travel both nationally and internationally. Always sharing about God’s transformational Life, our identity and authority within the Kingdom of God.

She has a passion to see women and men impacted by the living Word of God and His Presence. It is a delight to Jeanette to see people have the courage and excitement to journey with God to impact their family, their community, their city and ultimately our nation.

Jeanette has been married to Graeme for nearly 54 years. They have 3 children and 7 grandchildren.

Graham Renouf

Graham has served for many years as a pastor or lead associate pastor in New Zealand churches.

While serving as a pastor in the Wairarapa region of New Zealand he led his church in the revival that touched New Zealand in the 90s. That assignment prepared him for 11 years of serving as the lead associate pastor for a revival church in the Wellington region. In that role he released the lead pastor for international fanning the flames of revival while Graham kept the fires going at the local church.

Graham is strongly recognized pastoral gift of encouragement and helping people reach their potential is supported by an equally strong prophetic mantle. Graham has also helped pastors and churches throughout the midwestern part of the USA navigate the white waters of revival.

For the last ten plus years he has been asked by pastors to help them in managing the fires of revival that have been burning throughout the region. The anointing on his preaching and prophesying is supplemented by the way he is able to speak into the lives of both pastors and churches who are experiencing revival.

He has been called by one international evangelist as “New Zealand’s best kept secret”.

Tak Bhana

Tak is the Senior Pastor of Church Unlimited, a large multi-cultural church in West Auckland, New Zealand with seven campuses. He graduated from Auckland University in law and commerce and practised as an accountant before going into full time ministry.

Tak spent three years as a missionary in the Philippines and travels around New Zealand and the nations speaking at conferences, seminars and churches fulfilling his deep passion is to see the rapid growth and passion for the Holy Spirit, prayer, the Word of God, faith, winning souls and nations.

Tak hosts a national conference called New Zealand And Beyond which has a vision to equip leaders and churches to reach New Zealand and nations with the gospel. He also has a radio and television program called Running with Fire which broadcasts in New Zealand and many other nations.

His church produces a magazine with the same name which is distributed in over 70 countries. He is the author of the books: “Wired for the Supernatural and Power Unlimited". He is married to Adrienne and they have one married daughter and two grandchildren.

Kaylene Subritzky

As a non-Christian, Kaylene believed that there was no hope for her future. But God had His hand on her life and when she started to take her eyes off herself and began to thank God, her life was radically transformed. Kaylene’s book, God Has A Plan, talks about her journey from a dark place to finding new life in Christ. The miraculous way in which God called her into His Kingdom is a powerful testimony to His grace and love. Kaylene began working with New Zealand based Promise Keepers National office in 2010 and joined Charisma Christian Ministries as Deputy Director in 2012. Kaylene married Bill Subritzky in 2012 and together they conducted evangelistic outreach and healing meetings throughout New Zealand, in Australia, the Pacific and internationally.

Personal Statement
‘With the increasing stress and pressure of society, particularly over these last few years, we are all looking for ways to live meaningful lives. I have personally found the answers in God’s word and I know that the Bible is just as relevant today as it was 2000 years ago. Dove Ministries continues to be committed to walking with those in need so they can be set free to experience God’s love and purposes for their lives’.  Kaylene continues to lead Dove Ministries in the tradition started by Bill and Pat Subritzky over 45 years ago ministering in healing, deliverance, freedom and hope to those seeking the love of Jesus in their lives.

Len Buttner

Eagle Ascend Ministry is an itinerant prophetic teaching and equipping ministry, ministering nationally and internationally.

Len and Linda, the founders, have a passion and commitment to see individuals, churches and businesses fulfil their God given purpose and destiny by being healed in body, soul and spirit.
