Living from the Power of His Presence

In Aglow we treasure the presence of God. We know that it is in His presence that all that we need for our life flows. We are committed to pursuing the presence of God and following His leading. We recognise the power of His presence, not only the joy but the life changing encounter that comes from spending time in His presence. Our meetings are a place of Holy Spirit led worship, fellowship and the Word of God. Although we come from many varied backgrounds, we love the unity that comes from being children of the Kingdom of God with free access into the presence of our King and God.

If you are wanting to meet with like-minded followers of Jesus, then we would love for you to join us in His presence. Our local places of connection and times of meeting are shown on the connection button att he top of this page or just click below.
